About the Project

This project's research aims to create a computer system to automatically recognise emotions in children with high-functioning ASD to be used in future to support interventions for autism spectrum disorder treatment. This research was approved by both the NUI Galway’s Data Protection Officer and the NUI Galway’s Ethics Committee.
